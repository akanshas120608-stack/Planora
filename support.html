Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign In" button in the top right corner, then click "Sign up" to create a new account. You'll need a valid email address and a password (minimum 6 characters).

Do I need an account to use Planora?

You can view the interface without an account, but you'll need to sign in to save your tasks and subjects. Your data will be securely stored and synced across sessions.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link in your inbox.

Managing Tasks

How do I add a new task?

Click the "Add Task" button on any day card. Select the subject and enter your task description. Your task will be automatically saved to your account.

Can I edit my tasks?

Yes! Click directly on the task text to edit it. Changes are saved automatically when you click away.

How do I mark a task as complete?

Simply click the checkbox next to any task. Completed tasks will be visually marked with a strikethrough.

How do I delete a task?

Click the red trash icon next to any task. You'll be asked to confirm before the task is permanently deleted.

Managing Subjects

Can I customize my subjects?

Absolutely! Click "Manage Subjects" in the footer to add, edit, or delete subjects. You can customize both the subject name and color.

How do I add a new subject?

Go to "Manage Subjects" and click "Add New Subject". Enter the subject name and choose a color, then click "Save Subject".

Can I delete a subject?

Yes, but be careful! When you delete a subject, all tasks associated with that subject will also be deleted. This action cannot be undone.

How many subjects can I have?

On the free plan, you can have unlimited subjects! Premium features (coming soon) will add even more customization options.

Privacy & Security

Is my data safe?

Yes! Your data is stored securely using Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can other people see my tasks?

No. Your tasks and subjects are completely private and can only be accessed by you when logged into your account.

How do I delete my account and data?

Contact us at planorahelp12@gmail.com with your account email, and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Technical Issues

Planora isn't loading. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Refresh your browser (Ctrl/Cmd + R)
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  4. Check your internet connection
  5. If the problem persists, contact us at planorahelp12@gmail.com

My tasks aren't saving. Help!

Make sure you're logged into your account. Tasks require an active account to be saved. If you're logged in and still experiencing issues, please contact support.

What browsers does Planora support?

Planora works best on:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile)

Is there a mobile app?

Not yet! Currently, Planora is a web application that works on all devices through your browser. We're considering a mobile app for the future based on user demand.
