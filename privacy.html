Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2026

At Planora, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our study planning application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account or use Planora, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and encrypted password
  • User Content: Tasks, subjects, notes, and study plans you create
  • Waitlist Information: Email address if you join our Premium waitlist

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with Planora (features used, time spent)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Planora's services
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Store and sync your study plans and tasks across sessions
  • Send you important updates about our service
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Notify waitlist members about Premium feature launches
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, Google's cloud-based platform. Firebase provides:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Geographic data redundancy

3.2 Security Measures

We implement multiple security measures including:

  • Password Protection: Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • Secure Authentication: Firebase Authentication for secure user login
  • Data Access Controls: Strict database rules ensuring users can only access their own data
  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our active systems within 30 days.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Firebase/Google Cloud for hosting and data storage
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, safety, or property, or that of our users
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice)

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in Planora
  • Update or correct your account information
  • Delete your tasks, subjects, and study plans
  • Request account deletion at any time
  • Export your data (feature coming soon)

5.2 Communication Preferences

You can opt-out of promotional emails by contacting us at planorahelp12@gmail.com. However, we may still send you essential service-related communications.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Planora uses minimal cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions
  • Remember your preferences
  • Improve application performance

These are essential for the service to function and do not track you across other websites.

7. Children's Privacy

Planora is intended for users of all ages, including students under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Planora is operated from India. If you are accessing Planora from outside India, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India or other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Third-Party Links

Planora may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Planora after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

12. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to data processing
